ABOUT PNVSCA

Empowering Volunteerism for National Development

The Philippine National Volunteer Service Coordinating Agency (PNVSCA) is the government's lead agency responsible for promoting volunteerism as a strategy for nation-building, sustainable development, and active citizenship across the Philippines.

Community-Centered

Strengthening Filipino communities through meaningful volunteer engagement and collaborative initiatives.

Collaborative Partnerships

Connecting government agencies, institutions, organizations, and volunteers to achieve lasting social impact.

Nationwide Reach

Delivering volunteer programs and opportunities that reach communities throughout the Philippines.
